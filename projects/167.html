Encoder Launch (2024)

Flex Sensor, 9DOF IMU, PIC32

Encoder Launch is an innovative physics-based "launch-and-destroy" game that combines advanced sensor technology with interactive gameplay. Players control the shot power and angle of a digital projectile to strategically launch and hit targets. Leveraging components such as the flex sensor fitted to a glove, rotary encoder, IMU, PIC32 OLED display, and speaker, Encoder Launch offers a gaming experience that bridges the gap between physical interaction and digital gameplay.
